About Us

Established in 20201, we aim to use games as a medium to educate members on modern-day essential skills such as teamwork and strategic thinking.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to fostering a vibrant and inclusive community for all. Our mission is to provide an environment for students to develop modern-day essential skills such as Teamwork and Strategic Thinking by using games as a medium, allow them to build strong bonds with fellow students, represent the school in national inter-school games such as POL-ITE, and provide a medium for students to destress from the rigors of Polytechnic education.

Our History

NYP E-Sports was established in 2021 by students studying in all schools across the Polytechnic, with the goal of bonding students using popular games such as Valorant. Since then, we have grown to about 100+ members, participated and won multiple inter-school and national competitions, and most importantly, fostered teamwork among all of our members.

What We Do

We host CCA sessions weekly, and we often do:

  • Training Sessions: We host regular training sessions for a myraid of games such as Valorant, Counter-Strike 2, TETR.IO, and other games. We aim to build team chemistry, strengthen bonds between members, improve individual gaming skills and prepare for competitions.

  • Tournament Participation: Members from both the competitive and casual divisions often participate in inter-school or national E-Sports Competitions, representing their respective schools or NYP with pride. This allows our members to showcase their skills and talents to a wider audience, represent their respective schools or NYP as a whole with pride.

  • Community Events: We have hosted community events such as casual gaming events, where our CCA members can face off against other NYP Students outside of the CCA.

  • Skill Development: Using games as a medium, we impart essential modern-day skills to our members such as leadership, communication, teamwork, problem-solving and strategic thinking skills.
