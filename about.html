About Us

NYP E-Sports is an vibrant and inclusive community of student gamers, providing an platform for them to hone their skills and show off their talent. Since its founding in 2021, NYP E-Sports has become a place where gamers can interact and have fun with one other, regardless if you are just playing casually, or competitive gamers representing the school.

Our Mission

Our mission is to cultivate a supportive and competitive environment where students can develop valuable life skills through the medium of esports. We are dedicated to fostering teamwork, strategic thinking, and leadership while promoting sportsmanship, discipline, and academic excellence. By embracing the growing world of competitive gaming, we aim to build a community that values dedication, respect, and personal growth, preparing our members for success both in and out of the game.

Our History

The NYP E-Sports CCA started in 2021 with a simple idea: to bring students together through competitive gaming. What began as a small group of friends playing Valorant has evolved into a thriving community. We now compete in major leagues like Campus Legends Singapore and have a reputation for teamwork, sportsmanship, and success. Our club is more than just gaming; it's about building friendships, developing skills, and representing NYP with pride.


What We Do

We host CCA sessions weekly, and we often do:
Training Sessions: We run regular training sessions for games like Valorant, Counter-Strike 2, and TETR.IO for our club members to improve individual skills and team chemistry.
Tournament Participation: We form teams to participate in inter-school and national esports tournaments, proudly representing our school.
Community Events: We organize casual events for members to play against other students, building a wider gaming community within the school.
Skill Development: Beyond the games, our members learn crucial modern skills such as leadership, communication, and strategic thinking.
