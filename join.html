Join Our Esports Family!

Ready to take your gaming to the next level? Discover how you can become a part of our thriving esports community.

Why Join Us?


  • Develop Skills: Enhance your strategic thinking, reflexes, and communication skills in a competitive environment.

  • Teamwork & Camaraderie: Forge strong bonds with like-minded individuals and experience the thrill of victory as a team.

  • Represent Your School: Compete in inter-school tournaments and bring glory to our school in the exciting world of esports.

  • Professional Growth: Learn about the esports industry, event organization, and content creation.

  • Fun & Engagement: Enjoy regular gaming sessions, internal competitions, and social events with fellow enthusiasts.

How to Join?


  1. Express Interest: Fill out our online interest form below. This helps us understand your gaming preferences and availability.

  2. Attend an Info Session: We'll announce upcoming information sessions where you can learn more about the CCA, meet current members, and ask questions.

  3. Trial Sessions: Depending on the game, we may conduct trial sessions or tryouts to assess skills and team compatibility.

  4. Official Registration: Once selected, complete the official registration process with the school's CCA department.
