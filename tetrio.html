About Our Team

Our Tetrio team is a community of dedicated players who live for the thrill of competitive block-stacking. We focus on improving our stacking techniques, mastering T-Spins, and reacting at insane speeds. Whether you're aiming for a high TR (Tetra Rating) or just want to improve your APM, we provide a supportive environment for players of all skill levels.


Beyond the game, we're a close-knit group that shares a love for Tetris-style games. We hold regular practice sessions and community games to sharpen our skills and have fun together.
