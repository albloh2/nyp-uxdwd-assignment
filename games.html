Current Games

These are the official divisions of our CCA, with dedicated competitive teams and regular training sessions.


Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play tactical first-person hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games. It's a 5v5 game where teams must plant or defuse a spike, and players choose from a roster of agents with unique abilities.

CCA Focus: Competitive Team

Training: Tuesdays & Thursdays, 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM


Counter-Strike 2

The latest installment in the iconic Counter-Strike franchise, CS2 is a team-based first-person shooter where two opposing teams, Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists, compete in objective-based game modes.

CCA Focus: Competitive Team & Casual Play

Training: Mondays & Wednesdays, 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM


Mobile Legends

Mobile Legends is a 5v5 MOBA designed for mobile devices. It features fast-paced gameplay and a ranked system that allows players to compete against the best from around the world.

CCA Focus: Competitive & Casual Play

Training: Fridays, 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM
